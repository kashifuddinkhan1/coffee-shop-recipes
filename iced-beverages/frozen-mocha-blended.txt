Frozen Mocha Blended recipe:
-----------------------------

# Description: Standard Frozen Mocha Blended

# Ingredients:
1. 1 oz espresso
2. 1 cup almond milk
3. Blender
4. Optional sweetner
5. Ice cubes

# Steps:
1. Prepare espresso shot and pour into mug when ready.
2. Stir in sweetner (optional)
3. Place ice , milk, espresso, chocolate sauce in a blender. 
   Start at low speed and then high speed for 30 sec.
4. Pour blended mixture into 16oz glass.
5. Top with whipped cream and chocolate shavings.
