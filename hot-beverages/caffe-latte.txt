Caffe-Latte recipe:
------------------

# Description: Standard Caffe Latte

# Ingredients:
1. 1 oz espresso
2. 1 cup almond milk
3. Milk frother
4. Optional sweetner

# Steps to make coffee:
1. Prepare espresso shot and pour into mug when ready.
2. Stir in sweetner (optional)
3. Steam and froth milk until light layer of foam.
4. Gently pour the milk while holding back the foam.
5. Top with thin layer of foam remaining in the container.
